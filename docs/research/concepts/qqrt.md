# QQRT — Quantity, Quality, Regularity, Timing

**QQRT** is **[Matthew Walker](../entities/matthew-walker.md)**'s framing of good sleep as a chair
with **four legs**. Miss one leg and the chair tips — no single metric is sufficient.

| Leg | What it means | Practical target |
|---|---|---|
| **Quantity** | Enough total sleep | **7–9 hour range** (a band, not a fixed 8) |
| **Quality** | Continuity / efficiency of the sleep you get | **efficiency ≥ 85%** (asleep ÷ time-in-bed) |
| **Regularity** | Same bed/wake clock times day to day | see **[sleep-regularity](sleep-regularity.md)** (±15 min band) |
| **Timing** | Sleeping in phase with your own chronotype | wake/bed anchored to circadian preference |

## "3 things to start tonight"

Walker's most-impactful, lowest-friction starting set:

1. **Digital detox** before bed (the engagement problem, not the wavelength — see
   [sleep-debunks](sleep-debunks.md)).
2. **Regularity** — fixed wake/bed, weekends included.
3. **Light** — dim the evening environment (the wind-down layer).

## Mapping to mezo surfaces

- **Quantity + Timing** → the **sleep goal card** on the SleepPage: a duration target + a fixed
  wake/bed anchor derives the rest of the day ([`me.md`](../../features/me.md)).
- **Quality** → the **efficiency ring** (asleep ÷ in-bed ≥ 85%), fed by the enriched sleep log.
- **Regularity** → the **regularity ring** / score ([sleep-regularity](sleep-regularity.md)).
- **Light / "detox tonight"** → the **WindDownBanner** and night layer on Today
  ([`today.md`](../../features/today.md)), which nudge evening dim + digital detox at T-90/T-60.

Three of the four legs already ship as rings/goal; the fourth (the evening *light* practice) is the
wind-down/night surface. The **[debunks](sleep-debunks.md)** page records what QQRT explicitly is
**not** — the myths mezo builds nothing on.
