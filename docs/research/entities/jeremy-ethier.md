# Jeremy Ethier

**Jeremy Ethier** is a **kinesiology-based fitness educator** (co-founder of *BUILT WITH SCIENCE*),
known for YouTube videos that translate exercise- and sleep-science into practical routines. He is
mezo's source for the **morning half of the day** — the counterpart to
**[Matthew Walker](matthew-walker.md)**, who owns the sleep/evening science.

## Our captured source

Our raw layer for Ethier is his video **_"The Perfect Morning Routine to Build Muscle"_**
(`eifEiCYH2yc`), captured as
[extraction notes](../raw/transcripts/2026-07-23-ethier-morning-routine.md) on 2026-07-23. It is a
**single popular-education video** (not peer-reviewed primary research), hence `confidence: medium`.

## What we took from him

The video's **six recommendations** — circadian-aligned wake, morning sunlight, morning weigh-in,
caffeine timing/cutoff, morning training, and a protein-rich breakfast — are distilled in
**[morning-routine](../concepts/morning-routine.md)**, which maps each one to the mezo habit it
became (e.g. `morning_sunlight`, the caffeine cutoff, the morning weigh-in). Those habits live in the
habit engine documented in [`habit.md`](../../features/habit.md).

Critically, Ethier supplies exactly what Walker **leaves out**: the **caffeine numbers** (delay after
waking + a daily cutoff) and the **morning-light** content (Walker's light guidance is evening-only).
The two sources' clean division of labor is spelled out on the morning-routine concept page.
