# Matthew Walker

**Dr. Matthew Walker** is a professor of neuroscience and psychology at **UC Berkeley**, director
of its Center for Human Sleep Science, and author of the bestseller **_Why We Sleep_** (2017). He is
the most-cited popular voice on sleep science and the primary expert behind mezo's whole **Sleep
feature cluster**.

## Our captured source

Our raw layer for Walker is his **~2h17m interview on Steven Bartlett's _The Diary of a CEO_**
("Regularity is king", `qxxnRMT9C-8`), captured as
[extraction notes](../raw/transcripts/2026-07-23-walker-doac-sleep-interview.md) on 2026-07-23. It is
**one long-form interview**, not multiple independent sources — hence `confidence: medium`. The
numbers on the concept pages are quoted as he stated them; we have not re-derived them from the
underlying papers.

## What we took from him

Walker's organizing model is **[QQRT](../concepts/qqrt.md)** — Quantity, Quality, Regularity,
Timing — the four legs of good sleep. Three strands drive mezo:

- **[Sleep regularity](../concepts/sleep-regularity.md)** — "regularity is king"; the UK Biobank
  mortality numbers are why the app scores *consistency* of wake/bed, not just hours. This shipped in
  the Sleep goal + day-anchor (see [`me.md`](../../features/me.md)).
- **The practical evening/night layer** — wind-down dim (T-90, <30 lux, +18% REM), ~18 °C room, the
  20-minute rule, the "don't check the clock" calm-down toolkit, and sleep-banking. These became the
  wind-down/night surfaces and feed the routine habits in [`habit.md`](../../features/habit.md).
- **[Debunks](../concepts/sleep-debunks.md)** — an explicit list of sleep myths (smart-wake, blue
  light, magnesium, melatonin-as-supplement, Z-drugs) that mezo deliberately builds **no** features on.

He also flags what he **does not** cover — caffeine numbers, morning light, naps — which mezo instead
sources from **[Jeremy Ethier](jeremy-ethier.md)**'s morning-routine video. Together the two sources
partition the day: Walker owns the sleep/evening science, Ethier owns the morning routine.
